Features

Everything you need to
engage on LinkedIn.

Three tools in one extension. Works from the popup or directly inside LinkedIn's comment boxes.

💬
Comment Generator

Paste any LinkedIn post and get 3 tone-matched comments that add real value — not empty "Great post!" replies that get ignored.

✉️
Outreach Drafter

Cold DMs, connection requests, follow-ups, collaboration pitches — personalized to the person you're reaching out to.

↩️
Reply Generator

Someone messaged you — craft a reply that hits the right note, whether you're thanking, adding value, disagreeing, or closing.

✦
Inline AI Button

A floating ✦ button appears inside every LinkedIn comment box. One click drafts a comment from the post — without leaving the page.

🎯
Your Voice, Not AI's

Set your name, role, and bio once. Every generation adapts to sound like you — direct, warm, witty, or professional, your call.

🔒
Private by Design

Your posts and messages are processed and forgotten — nothing is stored after generation. Your LinkedIn password is never touched.

How it works

Up and running
in two minutes.

1
Install & sign in

2

Tell us your name, role, and a bit about your style. This is what makes the output sound like you instead of a robot.

3
Generate & copy

Paste a post, pick a tone, hit Generate. Choose the best of 3 options, copy it, and post on LinkedIn. Done.
